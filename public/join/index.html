Keep track of how much you spend

MyApp is a service that sends you one weekly text message telling you exactly how much, in dollar amounts, you spent during the last 7 days. It does so by securely retrieving transaction data from your bank* account through the use of Plaid. This means that MyApp never sees your bank login credentials, and thus is unable to keep them.

*Access supported to a wide range of financial institutions.

You will be able to login with either your number or your email.

Email will be used to send you monthly reports if you choose to sign up for them.

Use at least one letter, one numeral, and seven characters.
